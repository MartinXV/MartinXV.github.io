Redemption Tattoo is a relatively small shop located just before Porter square. Redemption doesn't boast many locations or many artists like the Somerville shops but it does offer quality work. While the styles may not vary as much you can get a good solid traditional or black and grey tattoo here.

Locations:Cambrdige
Artist:Jebb Riley and Adam LoRusso

If someone wanted a tattoo in Harvard square then the place to go is Chameleon Tattoo & Body Piercing. While not being that big, it constantly gets new and diverse customers who are either passing by or coming to study at the nearby University.

Locations:Cambrdige
Artist:Jared Scott and Joseph Boo
